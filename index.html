Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

Step 1/8: Raw Material

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

High-Precision Extrusion

Our extruders melt and homogenize the raw material, forcing it through precision-engineered dies to form a continuous pipe profile with consistent wall thickness.

  • Controlled melt temperature
  • Uniform wall thickness

Controlled Cooling Process

The extruded pipe passes through water cooling tanks at controlled temperatures to set the final dimensions and relieve internal stresses.

  • Gradual temperature reduction
  • Stress-free solidification

Precision Sizing & Calibration

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity throughout the pipe length.

  • ±0.1mm dimensional tolerance
  • Consistent outer diameter

Rigorous Quality Control

Each pipe undergoes hydrostatic pressure testing, dimensional checks, and visual inspection to ensure compliance with IS 5984, ISO 4427 and other applicable standards.

  • Hydrostatic pressure testing
  • ISO & BIS certified testing

Permanent Pipe Marking

Each pipe is permanently marked with product details including size, pressure rating, material grade, manufacturer information, and date of production for full traceability.

  • Full product traceability
  • UV-resistant ink marking

Precision Cutting to Length

Pipes are cut to standard or custom lengths using precision cutting machinery, ensuring clean square ends without deformation or burring.

  • Clean burr-free ends
  • Custom lengths available

Safe & Efficient Packaging

Finished pipes are bundled, wrapped, and palletised for safe transport, protecting them from UV, impact, and moisture during storage and delivery.

  • UV-protected wrapping
  • Export-ready palletising
